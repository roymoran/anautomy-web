Is it free?

Anautomy is free to use. As a user you are given limitless access to all car repair information. As a community driven platform, we only ask that you contribute to the platform for others to benefit.

How do I sign up?

Currently, you are only able to submit your email for access to our early launch. Invitations will be emailed to access the platform as we approach our early launch.

How do I contribute?

If you have ever gone through a car repair experience, we would love if you shared information about your experience. More information on how to share your experience is emailed once you submit your email to stay updated on our early launch.
