Is it free?

Anautomy is free to use. As a contributing member of our community, you are given limitless access to all car repair information including repair costs.

How do I sign up?

Currently, Anautomy is a closed community, but if you are interested in being a part of it please submit your email for access to our early launch. Invitations will be emailed to access the platform as we move towards opening our community.

How do I contribute?

If you have ever gone through a car repair experience, we would love for you to share it with our community. Submit your information, and we will reach out on sharing more information about your experience.
